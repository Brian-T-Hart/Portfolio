I am really glad you stopped by to check out ClassWorx. It is a favorite of mine. Class-Worx is the second of three group projects that I had the privilege of working on while at The Coding Bootcamp at UCI. I pitched the idea to the three other members of the group and they all got on board. ClassWorx is a resource for teachers to help in the gamification of the classroom. Teachers can create an account, add classes, and add students to classes. The students can be assigned points, homework passes, and hall passes. Teachers can sort the students by name or by points. They can also record notes for each student.

ClassWorx is the first full-stack project I worked on. We used Node.js, Mysql, Passport, Express, Handlebars, Html, CSS, Bootstrap, and Javascript. Though my initial role was focused on being the "Git-master", helping with back-end development, and getting the project live on Heroku, I can say I have a strong grasp of all aspects of the project. Since the project ended, I have continued to develop it. I have added new api routes in order to edit classes, edit student info, and reset all students for a particular class. I have fine tuned some database queries. I implemented Cloudinary, to make it easy for teachers to add student photos. I have created new pages, added modals, made css improvements, and more.
