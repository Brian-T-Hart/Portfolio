Click-Me-Game

Catch me if you can!

view site
view code

I came across this idea on another site and wanted to re-create it for fun and with my own twist. Using html, css, javascript, and jQuery, I made my own version with three speeds. The "click-me" text moves from square to square at random. If you click the square while the text is still in it, you earn a point. If you click the wrong box, two points are deducted from your score. You win the game when you get 10 points.

The easy level and the medium level can be played on most devices. The fast level works best on a computer with a mouse.
